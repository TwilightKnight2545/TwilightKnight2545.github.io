Prepping to be a Penny Pinching Pro

MJ Wickstrom

Money, it's important. It'd be great if we didn't need to worry about it, but as it stands, if you don't have money, you die, so it's something of a necessity. And in today's world, every company and their mother want's to get you to part with your hard-earned cash. One way to help hold onto your money better, is to create a budget.

In it's most basic form, a budget is simply a tool that tracks how much money you earn and how much you spend in a given period of time. If you earn more than you spend, you're doing alright. If you're spending more than you make, you should probably consider cutting down on uncessary spending.
