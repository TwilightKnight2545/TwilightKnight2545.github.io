Mindfulness: The ability to know what's happening in your head at any given moment without getting carried away by it.

Practicing mindfulness allows you to better handle negative stimuli and remain in control of your responses to stressful situations.

Journaling

One great way to improve your mental health and practice mindfulness is journaling. With so much happening in our lives, taking the time to sit down and write down your thoughts, feelings, and frustrations from the day can help you organize yourself. Journaling provides a way to get your thoughts out on paper, and it can help you reflect on your day. If your having a bad week, writing down your thoughts and feelings can help you identify and handle stress triggers. It can also help you prioritize any problems or goals you might have, and help you get closer to a solution. If your feeling adrift, try grabbing a pen and paper and taking ten minutes each day to write down the thoughts that are swirling in your mind.

Creative Outlets

Much like journaling, finding a way to creatively express yourself can be a wonderful method of improving your mental health in a variety of ways. Visual art methods such as drawing, painting, or woodworking can be a great way to use your hands, serving both as a creative outlet, and a form of meditation. Performing arts such as acting, dancing, or music are great forms of emotional release, and also provide the oppurtunity to interact with others and form a community around your shared interests. Creative writing, just like journaling, can also provide a way to express your thoughts and emotions in a safe environment. These are just a few examples, but there are dozens of different outlets that may suit you better!
